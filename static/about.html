LoopJoin is like LinkedIn, except your profile is stored on the MentoCoin Blockchain, and you maintain your own connections to others.

Actually, some data is stored directly on the Blockchain, and other data (like longer bio text, avatar images, etc) are stored in public Github repositories and simply linked to (via SHA1 git commit hashes) from transactions in the Blockchain.

Your profile is yours; it's not owned by any company. You decide what you disclose, and you decide who you connect with.
